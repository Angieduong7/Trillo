Hotel Las Palmas

8.6
429 votes

The Hotel Las Palmas, a 5 stars boutique hotel, offers 53 rooms uniquely decorated in blue shades. It has 11 standard rooms, 40 deluxe rooms and 2 suites, all equipped with free WIFI, air conditioning, direct telephone, slippers, bathrobe, magnifying mirror with light, double glazing, hairdryer, mini bar, 24 hour room service and interactive TV.

The guests of the Hotel Las Palmas can enjoy the restaurant located on the 8th floor, and offers a stunning view of the city, and great Portuguese meals. You can also stop at the "Rooftop Bar" located at the same building, on the 9th floor.
